Bio

I am a MSc student at the University of Toronto and Vector Institute under supervision of Prof Rahul G. Krishnan working on problems at the intersection of machine learning and healthcare. I was previously an undergraduate student at the University of British Columbia in Engineering Physics and an exchange student at ETH Zurich. During my undergrad I spent several terms working on implementing automated diagnosis algorithms for medical imaging with the UBC Robotics and Control Lab under supervision of Prof Purang Abolmaesumi.
